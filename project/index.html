About me

Greetings, I am Martin, 19 years old. This is my first year in the Sofia university and I am also trending as a junior programmer.

Highschool:

I wrote my first "Hello, world!" back in Highschool. I was 9th grade. I will never forget that day. The sun was shining, birds were singing and I was ... locked inside a room with a bunch of computers and a not so inspiring teacher. In Highschool i studied Java, HTML and CSS on a basic level. I didn't quite enjoy programming back then. I felt like the things we do were useless and have no practical use what so ever but at some point I met some people who got me into coding, people who brought value into these technologies, people who made programming useful for me.

The Software university:

When I was 11th grade I had accomplished everything there was to accomplish in Highschool but I wanted to improve so I starting searching for someone in school to teach me Object-oriented programming. I went through all of the school teacher but all of them refused to help me. A couple of months later I found a post about a trending software accademy called "The Software university". At first I was skeptical but I decided to give it a try. I finished level 0 and level 1 and I can say that this place gave me my programming fundamentals. Thexre I learned the basics of a couple of languages, frameworks and other technologies, I learned how to work in a team but most importantly i have learned how to manage problems I can't solve. A lot of time I was facing hard problems to which I had no solution but over time I learned how to start reserching the problems, gridnding it out and eventually solving it.
